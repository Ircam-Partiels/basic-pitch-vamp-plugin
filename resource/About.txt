The Basic Picth plugin is an implementation of the Basic Pitch automatic music transcription (AMT) library, using lightweight neural network, developed by Spotify's Audio Intelligence Lab as a Vamp plugin.

- Basic Picth Vamp plug-in by Pierre Guillot at Ircam (https://www.ircam.fr/)
- Basic Picth model by Spotify's Audio Intelligence Lab (https://research.atspotify.com/audio-intelligence/)
- Vamp by the Centre for Digital Music, Queen Mary, University of London (www.vamp-plugins.org)
